Frequently asked questions

Purchasing tickets through our website is a simple and secure process. First, browse our list of upcoming concerts and select the one you’re interested in. Once you’ve found it, click on the ‘Buy Tickets’ button. This will take you to a page where you can view a seating chart of the venue. Here, you can select the seats you prefer based on availability and price. After selecting your seats, you’ll be prompted to enter your payment information. We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and PayPal. Once your transaction is complete, you’ll receive a confirmation email with your ticket details. Remember to check your email’s spam folder if you don't see the confirmation in your inbox.

We understand that plans can change, but as per our policy, ticket sales are non-refundable. This is standard practice in the industry, as it allows us to ensure a stable and fair ticketing process for all customers. However, we do offer the option to transfer your ticket to someone else. If you find yourself unable to attend, you can change the name on the ticket for someone else to use. This process can be done by contacting our customer support team and providing them with the details of the new attendee. Please note that any such transfer should be completed at least 24 hours before the event.

If you haven’t received your tickets via email within 24 hours of your purchase, there are a few steps you can take. First, ensure that the email address you provided during the purchase process is correct. Next, check your spam or junk email folder, as sometimes emails from new addresses can be filtered there. If you still can’t find your tickets, please contact our customer service team with your order number and the name and email address used to purchase the tickets. Our team will investigate and re-send your tickets promptly. Your tickets are important, and we are committed to ensuring you receive them well in advance of the concert.

We’re thrilled to accommodate groups looking to enjoy a concert together! For groups of 10 or more, we offer special discount rates. This is a great option for corporate events, school trips, clubs, or even a large gathering of friends and family. To take advantage of this offer, please contact our group sales department. They will assist you with selecting seats, processing the payment, and providing all necessary information. Depending on the event, additional perks may be available for large groups, such as reserved seating or early entry. Please inquire with our group sales team for more details.

In the rare event that a concert is canceled or postponed, we prioritize communicating with all ticket holders immediately. If a concert is canceled, you are entitled to a full refund, including any service fees. The refund process is automatic and will be issued to the same method of payment used to make the purchase. Please allow a few business days for the transaction to process. If the concert is postponed, your ticket will remain valid for the rescheduled date. We will provide you with all the new details as soon as they are confirmed. If you cannot attend the rescheduled date, you will have the option to request a refund.

This technology encrypts your data, ensuring that it is secure and inaccessible to unauthorized parties. Additionally, we comply with all major security standards required for online transactions. Our website undergoes regular security audits to maintain these standards. We also have a dedicated team monitoring transactions to prevent fraud and unauthorized activities.
